Goals

This site intends to increase the number of customers of CossiePlay. This will also be an apportunity for the Client "Cossieplay" to promote their products and services to different types of audiences and expand its area of upportunity by introducing their company globally. This site will get customers to engage CossiePlay through phone or email for Party Registrations, Costume orders and Cossieplay Updates.

Success Evaluation

Success of this site can be measured by the increase of engagement between potential customers and the client through the given contact information on the site.

Target Audience

The site aims to get the attention of the young public ranging from Elementary to Secondary Students and even College Students who are interested on Cosplaying and turn them into customers of CossiePlay.

The site will be designed to look and sounds happy and playful for young audiences. This will express an environment that displays Cosplayers on their costumes and introduce the audience to a variety of costumes available at CossiePlay.
